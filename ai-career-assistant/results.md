# Results

## Overview

The AI Career Assistantdemonstrated how prompt engineering can improve the quality of career documents by transforming general AI responses into personalized, job-specific application materials.

---

## Keyword

### Personalized Cover Letter

By providing my resume and a specific job description, the generated cover letters reflected my own experience instead of producing generic content.

---

### Better Job Alignment

Refining the prompts helped generate the cover letters that matched the rquirements and keywords of the target job description more accurately.

---

### Increased Confidence

Receiving personalized and professional cover letters gave me greater confidence when submitting job applications after returning to the software industry.

---

### Most Effective Prompt Design

Iteratively improving the prompts by adding context, defining the AI's role , and specifying clear instructions resulted in more relevant and conistent outputs.

---

### Reflection

This project demonstrated that effective prompt engineering is not simply asking AI to write a cover letter. By providing clear context, defining objectives, and refining prompts through multiple iterations, I was able to generate application materials that were more authentic , personalized, and aligned with my career goals.
