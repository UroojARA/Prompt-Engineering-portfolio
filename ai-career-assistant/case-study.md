# Case Study

## Background

After nearly 10 years away from the software industry, I decided to return to the job market with a renewed focus on AI and Prompt Engineering . Although I had previous experience as a software developer, the long career gap made me feel like i was starting over . I needed to rebuild my professional profile, create new GitHub projects,  and present my skills in a way that reflected my current capabilities .

---

## Problem

Returning to the job market presented several challenges:
 - My resume no longer reflected current industry standards.
 - i had not updated my LinkedIn profile in years.
 - i was unsure how to write effective cover letters for diffrent roles.
 - i wanted to showcase new Prompt Engineering skills instead of relying on older software experience.
 - I needed a structured process to prepare professional application material affectively

---

## Objectives

The goal of this project was to design an AI Career Assistant capable of guiding a job seeker through the entire application process using prompt engineering.

Instead of generating generic responses , the assistant should create personalized and role-specific job document while maintaining a professional tone.

---

## Prompt Engineering 

The assistant follows a structured workflow:
 1. Analyze an existing resume.
 2. Modernize the resume using ATS-friendly formatting.
 3. Tailor the resume to a specific job description.
 4. Optimize the Linkedin profile.
 5. Generate a personalized cover letter.
 6. Create a professional recruiter outreach message.

This workflow demonstrates prompt chaining, context preservation, role prompting, and structured output generation.

---

## Expected Outcome

The AI Career Assistant helps job seekers create personalized career documents rather than generic AI-generated content

By tailoring resumes, cover letter, LinkedIn profiles, and outreach messages to specific opportunities, the assistant supports a more professional application process and improves the overall quality of job applications.
